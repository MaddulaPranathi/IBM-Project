# IBM-Project
Problem Statement :Course Content Simplification Agent

The Challenge 
Many students, especially first-generation learners and those from non-English medium backgrounds, struggle to understand complex academic course material. Textbooks, research papers, and lecture notes often use technical jargon or dense explanations, making it difficult for students to grasp core concepts independently. This comprehension gap leads to lower academic performance, reduced confidence, and a growing divide in learning outcomes — particularly in under-resourced or rural educational institutions. There is a pressing need for a solution that can automatically simplify educational content into clear, digestible formats suitable for a diverse student population.
The Objective 
Simplify Complex Academic Material Automatically convert textbooks, lecture notes, and research papers into clear, digestible explanations that are easy to understand.
Bridge Language & Comprehension Gaps Provide simplified content tailored for first‑generation learners and students from non‑English‑medium backgrounds.
Enhance Academic Confidence & Performance Support students in grasping core concepts independently, thereby improving learning outcomes and reducing dropout risks.
